Risk Bands (Fraud Score Thresholds)

HIGH Risk — fraud score ≥ 71

Customers with score at or above this threshold are treated as HIGH risk.

MEDIUM Risk — fraud score ≥ 31

Scores between this and HIGH threshold are treated as MEDIUM risk.

LOW Risk preview — fraud score ≤ 30

Derived automatically: anything below MEDIUM threshold is LOW risk.
